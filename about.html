About Us

Our Mission

Gender-Based Violence (GBV) Awareness is dedicated to raising awareness about the pervasive issue of violence against individuals based on their gender. Our mission is to educate, support, and advocate for survivors of GBV, and to work towards a world where everyone can live free from fear and violence.

What We Do

We provide resources, support, and education to help individuals and communities understand and combat gender-based violence. Our initiatives include:

  • Educational workshops and seminars
  • Support groups for survivors
  • Advocacy and policy work to influence legislation
  • Partnerships with local and international organizations

Our Team

Our team is composed of passionate advocates, educators, counselors, and volunteers who are committed to making a difference. We bring a diverse set of skills and experiences to our work, united by a common goal of ending gender-based violence.

Get Involved

We welcome individuals and organizations to join us in our fight against GBV. There are many ways to get involved, including volunteering, donating, and participating in our events. Together, we can create a safer, more just world for everyone.
