Gender-Based Violence Awareness

Awareness

Gender-based violence is a serious issue that affects millions of people worldwide. It is a form of violence that is directed towards an individual based on their gender, and it can take many forms, including physical, sexual, and emotional abuse.

It is important to recognize the signs of gender-based violence and to seek help if you or someone you know is experiencing it. You can also report incidents of gender-based violence to the appropriate authorities.
